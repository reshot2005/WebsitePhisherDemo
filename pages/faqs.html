Phishing Simulations Security Awareness Training
What is a phishing simulation?
Simulated phishing emails test employee awareness and are used alongside training to reduce risk.
Why use phishing campaigns?
They reveal weaknesses in human behavior and help organizations train before real attacks hit.
How does GoldPhish manage phishing campaigns?
We use automated tools and dashboards to create, deliver, and monitor phishing simulations with detailed analytics.
What is security awareness training?
It's a training program that teaches employees how to recognize threats like phishing, malware, and social engineering.
How often should training be done?
At least quarterly, with refreshers as needed based on threat level or employee role changes.
Can training be customized?
Yes. Training modules can be tailored to your industry, compliance needs, and internal policy requirements.
